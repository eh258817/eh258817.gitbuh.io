About

My name is Emma Hamilton and I'm a freelance artist from Ohio. Over the years, I've watched my art work go from barely interpretable scribbles on paper, to being skilled in multiple art mediums, primarily digital at the moment.

I've been doing some sort of creative outlet since I can remember, but quickly because particullay fond of digital drawing and painting, using this form of media to work on tattoo designs, t-shirt concepts for local business and much more

I've always lived by the belief that art can change the world. Especially during this time in our lives where so many people are silenced, what do they do? They express themselves in hope to chnmage the world, no matter who disagrees. So express ourself, be rebellious
